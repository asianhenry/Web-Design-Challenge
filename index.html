Champion Meta Diversity in Professional League of Legends



The purpose of this project was to analyze champion meta diversity in professional League of Legends. We will explore the campion meta within the 4 major regions LCS (North America), LEC (Europe), LPL (China), as well as an aggregate of all the regions. A score was calculated for each region on diversity scale from 0-100, 0 representing no diversity (the same 20 champions were present in every game) and 100 representing perfect diversity (each of the 148 champions were equally present among all the games played). Note that while only 10 champions are played in each game, bans are also included in the champsion presence.

The data represented here is for the spring 2020 regular season split for each region (does not include playoffs). The main factor we will look at is champion presence which is a percentage of how often the champion was picked or banned. The data will also show the most played and most banned champions in the current meta.
