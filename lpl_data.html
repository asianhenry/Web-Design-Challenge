Champion Meta Diversity: LPL



Here is the diversity graph for LPL in the Spring 2020 split. Among the 148 unique champions, 100 were played or banned at least once during the split. The diversity score for all LPL was 28.2 out of 100, only higher than LCS (region comparisons). There were 17 champions with an incredibly strong presence in the meta with at least a 50% presence. In order of presence, those champions include:

Aphelios, Sett, Elise, Renekton, Jarvan IV, Varus, LeBlanc, Olaf, Senna, Zoe, Thresh, Nautilus, Ornn, Miss Fortune, Kalista, Rumble, Lee Sin
