Champion Meta Diversity: LEC



Here is the diversity graph for LEC in the Spring 2020 split. Among the 148 unique champions, 107 were played or banned at least once during the split which was actually the largest number of unique champs across all regions (region comparisons). The diversity score for all LEC was 30.84 out of 100. There were 16 champions with an incredibly strong presence in the meta with at least a 50% presence. In order of presence, those champions include:

Aphelios, Gragas, Syndra, Ornn, Sett, Senna, LeBlanc, Pantheon, Rakan, Gangplank, Aatrox, Nautilus, Braum, Miss Fortune, Thresh, Lee Sin
